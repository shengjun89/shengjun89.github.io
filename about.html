About me

I’m an award-winning Product Designer working at the intersection of design and technology.

What began as a curiosity to create and build things evolved into a profession that I’m passionate about. I’m proud to have worked with some of the leading companies since 2014, designing digital products and services that millions use in their daily lives, from planning their public transport routes to sending parcels across the world.

My work as a product designer involves collaborating with cross-functional teams to identify and solve complex problems, from ideation to implementation. Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to work on a variety of projects, from consumer mobile apps to web platforms and enterprise software.

I believe in

Doing what’s promised

Born in Finland, I have been raised with a cultural emphasis on outspokenness and straightforward communication. When I make a promise, you can trust that I will do my best to deliver.

Lifelong learning

The phrase "perpetual learning machine" from the Almanack of Naval Ravikant really resonates with me and describes my approach to life and work. If there's something I don't know, I'll make it a point to learn and apply it.

Helping others to succeed

Throughout my career, I've been fortunate to have excellent people supporting me, and I want to pay it forward. I've had the opportunity to help people transition from development to design and have mentored others on topics such as career planning, building a design portfolio, and freelancing.
